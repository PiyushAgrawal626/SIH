Predictor

Our AI model analyzed the daily electricity usage in Delhi for the last one year. It looked at patterns and trends in consumption. Additionally, we fed weather data as an input. Now, armed with this information, the AI can predict how much electricity will be needed on any given day.This data is very useful for Delhi government as this data can be used to provide the Delhi government thus reducing the wastage of power.

Graph Analysis

Our data-driven visualizations will provide a clear analysis of electricity consumption patterns in Delhi. Line charts will display daily trends, showing increases during hot summer days, decreases during rainy periods, and variations between weekdays and weekends. Scatter plots will correlate weather conditions with electricity usage, identifying the impact of temperature on air conditioning and humidity on cooling systems. Side-by-side comparisons will present AI predictions alongside actual consumption, highlighting areas where the model performs well and where it needs improvement. These visualizations will guide effective energy management.
